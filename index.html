Strengthening the Ecosystem Behind Creativity

At New Roots India, we exist to strengthen the ecosystem behind creativity. Founded in 2018, we are built on a simple yet powerful belief: when talent is supported by the right systems, it can truly flourish.

The entertainment industry is dynamic, fast-paced, and often overwhelming. Behind every successful artist or seamless production lies a complex web of contracts, schedules, finances, compliance, and operations.

We act as the backbone support for artists, creators, and production houses—quietly managing the business, logistics, and safeguards that allow creativity to take center stage.

"While you focus on the art, we ensure the foundation beneath it is strong, secure, and stress-free."
